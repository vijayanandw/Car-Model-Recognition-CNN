What Powers It

Built on proven deep learning techniques, fine-tuned specifically for automotive classification.

🧠

Transfer Learning

MobileNetV2 pre-trained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on 16,185 car images to maximize classification accuracy with minimal compute.

🎯

196 Vehicle Classes

Stanford Cars Dataset spanning 196 categories — from the Acura TL 2012 to the Volvo C30 2012, covering major manufacturers worldwide.

⚡

Real-Time Inference

Lightweight MobileNetV2 architecture enables sub-second predictions on CPU, with full confidence score output for every classification.

🔬

OpenCV Preprocessing

Images are normalized, resized and passed through an optimized preprocessing pipeline before reaching the model to ensure reliable results.
